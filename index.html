What's This Cookie All About?

Pat's Famous Salmon Cookies are a well-known Seattle tradition since 2014. And soon to be reknown world-wide. We use a trademarked method of grinding dry smoked-salmon down into infintesable amounts and mix it with some of the Pacific Northwest's finest ingredients to blend together the finest cookie ever produced. When enjoyed with some of the finest coffee selection known to man, Famous Pat's Salmon cookies are the most delicious treat imaginable.
